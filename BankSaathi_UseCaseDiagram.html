The five agents — Samjho (understand & qualify), Khojo (scheme discovery), Chalao (YONO navigation), Queue Hatao (proactive engagement), and Suraksha (fraud & protection) — operate inside one orchestrated system. Tier-3 actions such as loan sanctioning stay with the Branch Officer; the agent only pre-briefs and escalates. Account data access is gated by consent and authentication.
